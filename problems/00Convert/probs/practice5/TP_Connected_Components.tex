\begin{problem}

%% type: short-answer
%% title: Connected Components

Let $G$ be the graph whose vertices are the integers,

with an edge between $i$ and $j$ iff $|i - j| = 6$.

How many connected components does $G$ have?

\begin{solution}

6

The vertex sets of the 6 components are
\begin{align*}
& \{\dots -18, -12, -6, 0, 6, 12, 18,\dots \} \\
& \{\dots -17, -11, -5, 1, 7, 13, 19,\dots \} \\
& \{\dots -16, -10, -4, 2, 8, 14, 20,\dots \} \\
& \{\dots -15, -9, -3, 3, 9, 15, 21,\dots  \} \\
& \{\dots -14, -8, -2, 4, 10, 16, 22,\dots \} \\
& \{\dots -13, -7, -1, 5, 11, 17, 23,\dots \}
\end{align*}
\end{solution}
